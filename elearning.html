by Evlyn Mwihaki Muketha CIT/00274/019 - Saturday, 12 June 2021, 5:08 PM
ADVANTAGES OF THE RAPID DIAGNOSTIC TEST ARE:
a)They enable decentralization of HIV testing and counseling-it allows HIV testing and counseling to be decentralized to community services away from major urban centers.
b) Acceptability to clients- many people prefer services where they can receive their test result without delay therefore there has been an increased demand after rapid testing was introduced.
c)Reduced cost-Rapid test algorithm leads to a greater proportion of clients receiving their test results ,reduced wastage of test kits and increased efficiency in comparison to other testing strategies , testing algorithms based on rapid tests have a lower cost per patient receiving results.
d)They are simpler to perform and are visual tests that do not require the ELISA Reader.
LIMITATIONS OF RAPID DIAGNOSTIC TEST ARE:
a)Rapid tests tend to be have more sensitivity and specificity comparable to ELISA.
